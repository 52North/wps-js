wps-js testclient for comparison of classic and restful responses

Clicking one of the buttons will cause the wps-js library to make the very same call to both a classic, XML-based WPS and a restful, JSON-based WPS proxy. The response objects the library returns to the end user after the calls are finished will be displayed below: on the left is the classic version, on the right the restful version. See also comment in src/web/js/wps-js-lib/response/ExecuteResponse_json.js. Contact: Christoph Friedrich
